To Lay Awake Under the Stars
to lay awake under the stars

is to be feeling brave, feeling lucky
more like getting lucky
in the safety of a good friend's arms

we're brave and lucky to be here
to have made it this far

it takes a certain kind of recklessness to get where we are
and that's kind of beautiful

but it's also small, and sad
and pointless
but we're here, so we've got to do it!

and we take rests in the crux between infinity and infinitely small
sipping on root beers
which are expertly paired with summer romance
and the starry sky
